About KSP

Our Story

Founded in the heart of Atlanta, Georgia in 1994, Kevin Styles and Psalms (KSP) is a ministry born from a contemporary gospel choir gifted singers and instrumentalists. is a contemporary gospel choir known for their uplifting and inspirational music and ministry. The choir, led by Kevin Styles, aims to inspire through music and community engagement, traveling nationwide to perform and minister to various communities. They are dedicated to delivering powerful performances and heartfelt messages. The choir has a rich history of collaborating with established figures in the gospel genre. Psalms has provided background vocals and collaborated directly with renowned artists such as: Kirk Franklin, the late Lashaun Pace, Isaac Caree, and Bishop Hezekiah Walker. They have also been featured on prominent platforms, including a notable appearance at Verizon's How Sweet the Sound competition in 2013, which served as a major showcase for their exceptional performance ability.

Our Mission

Our mission is to use the universal language of music to share the Gospel, encourage believers, and inspire hope. We blend the rich, traditional sounds of Gospel with modern Contemporary Christian and R&B influences to create an authentic worship experience that transforms hearts.

Meet Kevin Styles

Kevin Styles is the visionary founder and director, whose innovative arrangements drive the KSP sound. His leadership ensures that every performance is delivered with passion, precision, and purpose, establishing him as one of Atlanta's most respected gospel leaders.
